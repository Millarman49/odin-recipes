Sean's Spag Bol

Description

A good spaghetti bolognese or "Spag Bol" as it's known to the refined man may be simple but can be life changing.

Disclaimer: This recipe is not claiming to be life changing and any change to your or relatives lives as a result of this recipe are purely coincidental and not the responsiblity of the recipe author

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion
  • 1 pack of closed cup mushrooms
  • 1 pack of 5% fat beef mince or supermarket brand frozen veggie mince
  • Pasta - Spaghetti or Rigatoni
  • Extra Mature Cheddar Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Oregano
  • 1 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • Worcester Sauce

Steps

  1. Peel and slice the red onion, and chop the mushrooms
  2. Add the red onion to a frying pan with a little oil and fry for a couple mins on high heat
  3. Add mushrooms to the same pan and cook until soft
  4. Once the mushrooms are soft, add the mince to the pan and break into smaller chunks if needed so it is easier to cook.
  5. Continue to cook until the mince has browned all over.
  6. Boil the water for the pasta
  7. Add pasta to the boiling water with a pinch of salt and stir in.
  8. Add tin of chopped tomatoes to the mince, 1 tbsp of tomato puree and stir together until it is all covered.
  9. Add a 1 tsp of orgeano and 1 tbsp of worcester sauce to the mince and stir together
  10. Reduce the heat to medium and allow to simmer and cook on low heat for 10 mins
  11. Once the pasta has cooked (10-12 mins), drain it and add portion into large bowls
  12. Add spoonfuls of bolognese generously to the top of each portion of pasta
  13. Add grated cheddar cheese on top and serve (Parmesan can be used instead if desired)
