Mac 'n' Cheese

Description

Possibly the greatest or easiest mac n cheese recipe, it's at least 1 of the 2. Utilising the gourmet Colmans cheese sauce packet, this recipe is a quick and optional ingredients such as bacon and crispy onions can be used to "dress up" this recipe as needed

Ingredients

  • Colmans Cheese Sauce Packet
  • 300ml Milk (Skimmed, Semi or whole
  • Pasta (Spirali, Macaroni or an alternative of your choice
  • Broccoli
  • Extra Mature Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water for pasta
  2. Whilst water is boiling, chop broccoli
  3. Once water has boiled, pour desired amount of pasta into water and add a pinch of salt.
  4. Add broccoli to the same saucepan as the pasta.
  5. Pour cheese sauce powder into a separate saucepan
  6. Pour a small amount of the milk into the saucepan with the powder and stir until it is a paste like consitency
  7. Gradually add more milk whilst stirring until all of the milk has been used, if the sauce becomes too watery then stop pouring and keep stirring until paste like consistency has been achieved.
  8. Once the pasta has cooked (10-12 mins), drain it and add it to the sauce.
  9. Combine sauce, pasta and broccoli
  10. Dish up and serve with grated cheddar on-top
  11. Optional: Place mac 'n' Cheese to oven-proof dish top with cheese and grill until cheese is golden brown
